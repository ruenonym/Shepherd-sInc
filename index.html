SHYAM is a gaddi shepherd who was born and brought up in the lap of the himalayas. He spent more than 8 years serving his family as a shepherd before venturing into the field of mountaineering and trekking. He is trained at Atal Bihari Vajpayee Institute of Mountaineering and Allied Sports under the qualification of Basic + Advance Muntaineering Course and MOI [Method of Instruction]. He is also qualified at Search & Rescue from Nehru Institute of Mountaineering.
When he is not in the wildnerness he prefers to spend time at the Regional Mountaineering Institute as an instructor educating children from various government schools.
As a shepherd, he believes that Shepherding as a dying tradition is causing a lot of indeginous knowledge to fade away from the Gaddi community. This initiative is an effort to revive their nomadic culture and the various activities and artforms asssociated with it.
